DigiWater

Hydraulic Modeling and Simulation Platform

DigiWater is a web-based hydraulic modeling and visualization platform, designed for engineers, researchers, and policymakers to simulate and analyze complex water systems. Whether you’re studying flood risks, coastal dynamics, or riverine flows, DigiWater provides high-fidelity simulations in an accessible, user-friendly interface
